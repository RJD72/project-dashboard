Card Components

The card UI element is designed to present content in a visually appealing and structured way. It consists of a header, content, and footer sections, each with customizable styles and content. Cards are built with as little markup and styles as possible, but still manage to deliver a ton of control and customization. They offer easy alignment and mix well with other components.To use the card UI element, simply include the HTML structure provided below within your project.

Usage

These card components can be used throughout your website to present content in a visually appealing and structured manner. You can customize the colors, titles, and content to suit your specific needs and branding.
