StarWing Airways Typeface and Font Usage

At StarWing, font usage is a crucial aspect of your design, and it plays a significant role in conveying your message effectively. Below, you'll find comprehensive guidelines and code snippets related to font usage for various elements, aligning with your branding and business goals while ensuring a consistent and recognizable look on your website.

Headers and Menus

For your website headers and menus, we recommend using the Varela font. It complements your brand identity and aligns with the clean and modern aesthetic you're aiming for.

        
font-family: 'Varela', sans-serif;

Header Text:

For the header text, we suggest using the Varela font with a font size of 36px and a font weight of 700 (Bold). This combination will make your headers stand out and create a strong visual hierarchy.

        
font-family: 'Varela', sans-serif;
font-size: 36px;
font-weight: 700;

Menu Items:

Menu items should also use the Varela font. To ensure clarity and ease of navigation, set the font size to 18px and the font weight to 500 (Medium).

        
font-family: 'Varela', sans-serif;
font-size: 18px;
font-weight: 500;

Body Content

To ensure readability and a professional look for your main content, the Roboto font is an excellent choice.

        
font-family: 'Roboto', sans-serif;

Main Content Text:

For main content text, use the Roboto font. To strike a balance between readability and a clean, modern look, set the font size to 16px and the font weight to 400 (Regular).

        
font-family: 'Roboto', sans-serif;
font-size: 16px;
font-weight: 400;

Background

The background color we suggest is True White from your chosen color palette. It will provide a clean and fresh backdrop for your content.

        
background-color: #FFFFFF; /* True White */

Chart Data

For chart data, we recommend using a serif typeface to add a touch of sophistication. To maintain legibility and balance, use the Times New Roman font.

        
font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif;

Chart Data Text:

For text within your chart data, maintain a font size of 14px and a font weight of 400 (Regular). This ensures that the data is presented clearly and professionally.

        
font-size: 14px;
font-weight: 400;

By following these detailed font usage guidelines and incorporating the provided code snippets, you can create a visually appealing and coherent design for your website that effectively communicates your brand identity and mission.
