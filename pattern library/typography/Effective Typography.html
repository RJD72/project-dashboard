StarWing Airways Typeface and Font Stack

At StarWing, we understand the importance of visual consistency in your branding and design. To ensure your website embodies the essence of "Clean, new, and fresh," we've carefully selected a Typeface and font stack for your Typography needs. Below, you'll find examples and code snippets to help you visualize how these choices will look on your website.

Headers and Menus

For your website headers and menus, we recommend using the Varela font. It complements your brand identity and aligns with the clean and modern aesthetic you're aiming for.

        
font-family: 'Varela', sans-serif;

Body Content

To ensure readability and a professional look, the Roboto font is an excellent choice for your main content.

        
font-family: 'Roboto', sans-serif;

Background

The background color we suggest is True White from your chosen color palette. It will provide a clean and fresh backdrop for your content.

        
background-color: #FFFFFF; /* True White */

Chart Data

For chart data, we recommend using a serif typeface to add a touch of sophistication. However, it's essential to maintain legibility and balance. Here's a recommended serif typeface stack:

        
font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif;

Font Size and Weight

To maintain a consistent look across your website, we suggest balancing the font size and weight. Ensure that your titles and chart data have a coherent visual hierarchy.

        
font-size: 16px;
font-weight: 400; /* Regular */

By following these recommendations and incorporating the provided code snippets, you can achieve a visually appealing and coherent design for your website.
